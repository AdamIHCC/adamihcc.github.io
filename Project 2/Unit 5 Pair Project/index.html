The Microwave Made By Gamers

Our Story

We started out as two ordianry gamers, when one day we realised, we didn't have a gamer microwave. That had to change.

Our Product

Using RGB technology, we developed a working prototype of a gamer microwave. This microwave featured RGB lights in the buttons of the microwave. And eventually, the inside of the microwave will have RGB lighting too.

Our Goal

We want to give gamers more opportunities to be gamers in their everyday lives. Since we all know gamers can't cook, we know the gamer microwave will be a welcome addition to any gamers gaming cave.
