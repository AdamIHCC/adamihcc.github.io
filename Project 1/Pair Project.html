Morbin Morbius' Comic Origins

Dr. Micheal Morbius is a brooding, tragic character with a rare blood disease. While attempting to cure himself, he instead imbued himself with pseudo-vampiric super powers. Since then he has struggled to uphold his oath as a docotor and survive as a vampire lusting for human blood.

  • First appearing in The Amazing Spider-Man #101 (October, 1971), Morbius debuted as a horror-based villain for Spider-Man.
  • Soon after his introduction, Morbius became an adversary to the vampire hunter, Blade.
  • In the 1990s Morbius recieved his own comic issues, detailing his tragic backstory as a world-renowned biochemist.
